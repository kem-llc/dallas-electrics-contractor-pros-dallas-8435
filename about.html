Our story

Your Trusted Dallas Electrician Since 2011

Our mission is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable electrical services to every customer, treating your home or business as if it were our own.

Dallas Electrics Contractor Pros was founded in 2011 by master electrician John Davis, who saw a need for honest, high-quality electrical work in the Dallas area. Starting with just a truck and a commitment to excellence, John built a reputation for reliability and craftsmanship. Over the years, we have grown into a team of licensed professionals serving thousands of satisfied customers across Dallas and surrounding communities.

Today, we continue to uphold the same values that started it all: transparency, quality, and customer care. Every electrician on our team undergoes rigorous training and background checks. We invest in the latest tools and technology to ensure efficient, safe service. Whether it's a simple outlet repair or a complex commercial installation, we approach every job with the same dedication. We're proud to be rated 4.8 stars and trusted by our neighbors.
