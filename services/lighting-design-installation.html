Overview

Great lighting enhances ambiance, improves safety, and reduces energy costs. Our lighting services include indoor and outdoor design, LED retrofits, landscape lighting, security lighting, and smart home integration. We work with you to create a lighting plan that matches your style and budget. Our electricians ensure every fixture is installed correctly and efficiently. Whether you want to highlight architectural features or brighten a workspace, we bring your vision to light.

Common questions

How much can I save with LED lighting?

LEDs use up to 80% less energy than incandescent bulbs and last 25 times longer. Most customers see a return on investment within 1-2 years through lower utility bills.

Do you install smart lighting systems?

Yes! We install and configure smart lighting from brands like Lutron, Philips Hue, and others. Control your lights from your phone or set schedules for convenience and security.
