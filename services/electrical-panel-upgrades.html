Electrical Panel Upgrades

Upgrade your electrical panel for safety, capacity, and peace of mind.

Schedule this service

Overview

Your electrical panel is the heart of your home's electrical system. If it's outdated, overloaded, or unsafe, it can pose serious risks. We specialize in panel upgrades that increase capacity, improve safety, and meet modern electrical demands. Our electricians handle everything from 100-amp to 400-amp upgrades, including service entrance replacements. We use top brands like Square D, Eaton, and Siemens, and our work comes with a full warranty. Protect your home and family with a professional panel upgrade.

What's included

  • Eliminate breaker trips and flickering lights
  • Support for modern appliances and electronics
  • Compliance with current electrical codes
  • Increased home safety and resale value

How it works

1

Inspection & Load Calculation

We assess your current panel, calculate your home's electrical load, and determine the right upgrade size. This ensures your system meets your needs.

2

Permitting & Planning

We handle all necessary permits and plan the upgrade to minimize downtime. You'll get a clear timeline and cost estimate.

3

Installation & Testing

Our licensed electricians perform the upgrade safely and efficiently. We test every circuit, label the panel, and clean up thoroughly.

Common questions

How long does a panel upgrade take?

Most residential panel upgrades take 4-8 hours. We work efficiently to restore power as quickly as possible, often the same day.

Do I need a permit for a panel upgrade?

Yes, panel upgrades require a permit and inspection to ensure code compliance. We handle all permitting and scheduling for you, so you don't have to worry.
