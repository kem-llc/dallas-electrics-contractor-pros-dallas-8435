Common questions

How do I know if my electrical panel needs upgrading?

Common signs include flickering lights, frequent breaker trips, buzzing sounds, or if your panel is over 25 years old. We offer free inspections to assess your panel's condition.

Do you offer emergency residential service?

Yes! We provide 24/7 emergency service for issues like power outages, sparking outlets, or downed wires. Call (214) 206-8435 anytime.
